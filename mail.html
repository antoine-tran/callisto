Mailing List

A mailing lists has been set up for the Callisto community, hosted by The MITRE Corporation [1]. To reduce SPAM, posting to the callisto-users-list has been restricted to list members.

Bug reports and technical questions can be posted to the mailing list; however, as Callisto is not currently being actively supported, you may not receive a reply from the development team.

callisto-users-list
List where users and developers join to discuss questions, problems, features, and all other present and future aspects of Callisto. You must subscribe to the mailing list before you can post messages.

[1] Views expressed on the mailing lists are, unless explicitly stated otherwise, the views of the poster only, and do not necessarily reflect the views of the MITRE Corporation, the Callisto team, or other users of Callisto.

Subscribing

To join a list, send an email message to <listserv@mitre.org> with the following line in the body of the email (The subject line is ignored by the listserv). 

    SUBSCRIBE listname yourname

For example, if John Smith wanted to join the discussion, he would send the following command in the body of his email to listserv@mitre.org: 

    subscribe callisto-users-list John Smith

Posting

To send messages to the user list, send mail to <callisto-users-list@lists.mitre.org>. Please choose a short, yet descriptive subject for your message.

Unsubscribing

To unsubscribe from a list, send an email message to <listserv@mitre.org> with the following line in the body of the email from the same account you subscribed from. (Again: the subject line is ignored) 

    UNSUBSCRIBE listname

For example, if John Smith decided wanted to unsubscribe from the user list, he would send the following command in the body of his email to listserv@mitre.org: 

    unsubscribe callisto-users-list

If you have trouble unsubscribing from a list due to a change in email address, or some other problem, contact the list administrator to be removed.
